Weather App

A JavaScript application that provides you with the current weather conditions and 5 day forecast of any city that you may desire. It also includes, but it is not limited to, the following features: time, date, current temperature, "feels like" temperature, wind speed, humidity percentage, daily high & lows, search bar, & a geolocation button.
The colors that inspired the palette of this creation were those of the sunsets if often watched while I lived next to the beach near Los Angeles.
